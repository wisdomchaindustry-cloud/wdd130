Akwa Ibom State

Akwa Ibom is a state in the south-south region of Nigeria. It is known for its rich culture, scenic landscapes,and economic significance. With lush rain forests, rivers, and an Atlantic coastline, it thrives in agriculture, fishing, and tourism. The people are renowned for their hospitality and vibrant traditions, showcased in festivals like the Ekpo and Ibibio masquerades. A key contributor to Nigeria's oil and gas industry, Akwa Ibom blends modern urban development in Uyo and Eket with enduring cultural heritage.
